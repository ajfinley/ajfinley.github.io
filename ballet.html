ABOUT

Born in Santa Clarita, California, Alexandria Finley began dancing at age 3 at Santa Clarita Ballet Academy under the direction of Corinne Glover and Carol Guidry. She trained in the Royal Academy of Dance syllabus, and holds her Advanced 2 certificate with Distinction. She continued her training with Berkeley Ballet Theater, under the direction of Sally Streets and Robin Dekkers, while pursuing her engineering degree at the University of California, Berkeley. While dancing with BBT, she enjoyed performing roles such as the Sugar Plum Fairy in Streets' The Nutcracker and the principal woman in Paquita.
